Specificity is an Algorithm that is used by browser to determine the css declaration that is most relevant to on element,which in turn, determine the property value to apply to element.

"Basically Specificity is an algorithm that calculate the weight of css Selector and apply to element."

Selector Specificity Value Calculation
* 0 0
p 1 1
p.text 11 1 + 10
p#demo 101 1 + 100
#demo 100 100
.demo 10 10
attribute 10 10
pseudo 10 10
inline Style 1000 1000
!important 10,000 10,000
